Meet Nelson 🐾

The golden retriever behind every card — and the reason his humans haven't had a quiet morning since 2023.

The dog. The brand. The Belfast legend.

Nelson is a golden retriever living his best life in Belfast, Northern Ireland. He's enthusiastic about walks, suspicious of the postman, and completely unaware that he's become a small business.

The shop started because the cards didn't exist — not the right ones, anyway. Hand-drawn golden retrievers, for golden retriever people. So we made them. Nelson approved (he sniffed the first print run and wandered off, which we're taking as a yes).

Every card is printed fresh on 350gsm premium card stock, tucked into an envelope, and packaged with care. We're not a print-on-demand warehouse — we're one dog's household, packing orders at the kitchen table.

592 sales, 5.0★, and counting. Nelson takes no credit but accepts all treats.

Packaging is part of the product

Our customers notice the packaging — it's one of the things they mention most in reviews. Each order arrives beautifully presented, with a golden retriever sticker sealing the envelope. It's the kind of thing you want to receive, not just something to tear open.
